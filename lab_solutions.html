Adding pipes

In the prompts above, we iteratively overwrote the sales dataset each step. This can get clunkyâ€”instead we could pipe together the above prompts. Give it a try!

homes[, .(ym_sold, SalePrice)][
  ym_sold >= "2007-12-01" & ym_sold <= "2009-06-01"][
    , .(avg_price = mean(SalePrice)), by = ym_sold][
      order(ym_sold)]
